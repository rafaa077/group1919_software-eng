How to Make Iced Coffee

  • Brew a strong cup of coffee and let it cool.
  • Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  • Add milk, cream, or sweetener to taste.
  • Stir well and enjoy!
